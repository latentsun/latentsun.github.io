About the project

This was a TV spot for LG’s ThinQ AI range, produced with Brand & Deliver. I modelled the TV from reference photos, then textured, animated and rendered everything in Cinema 4D with Octane. The core idea was to take the on-screen AI responses and turn them into a flowing mograph stream that sweeps across the TV, guiding the eye and finally revealing the logo as it drifts back. Matching the UI interactions with the movement was a really fun challenge, and made the whole piece feel alive.
