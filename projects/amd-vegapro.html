About the project

A cinematic YouTube trailer for AMD’s Vega launch, created with Brand & Deliver. I built a series of 3D scenes around the iconic Vega symbol, starting with close-up bokeh shots in branded blue metal for a sleek, tech-driven tone. The narrative then moves into a moody warehouse reveal, combining keyed silhouettes, Houdini smoke simulations imported as .vdb files, and carefully balanced lighting in Octane to keep everything atmospheric. The final shot unveils the Vega icon floating like an alien artifact, with subtle X-Particles adding an ethereal shimmer. Balancing light levels in a physically-accurate renderer was a real challenge, but I’m thrilled with how the whole piece came together.
