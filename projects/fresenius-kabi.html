About the project

This looping key art was created for a Huawei conference. The idea was for a glass dragonfly to emerge from the Huawei logo, take flight, interact with others, and return to land seamlessly. I modelled the dragonfly in Blender, then rigged, animated, and rendered it in Cinema 4D with Octane. The hardest part was getting the wings to feel fast enough to read as a dragonfly without vanishing completely into blur — it took a lot of fine-tuning to strike that balance between realism and visibility.
