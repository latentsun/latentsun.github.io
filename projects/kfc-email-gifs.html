About the project

A suite of looping animated GIFs for a KFC email campaign, created with Iris. I built all the motion in After Effects, then compressed and optimised everything in Photoshop for email delivery. One standout piece features hand-drawn flames — a fun challenge and a nice break from my usual motion-graphics workflow. It was great to bring a bit of character and warmth into something so small and tightly constrained.
