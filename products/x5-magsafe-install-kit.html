Installation

Remove 12V socket from dash.

Remove +/- terminals from 12V socket and replace with included spade terminals.

Reattach factory +/- terminals to the spade terminals. (Centre +/ Sleeve -)

Route custom cable through the hole behind the factory ashtray.

Attach +(Red)/-(Red/Black) to the spade terminals.

Plug MagSafe Charger into the USB-C port on the custom cable.
