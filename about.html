About Latentsun

Design, 3D printing, software, and practical problem-solving.

Latentsun is an independent creative and technical studio focused on motion graphics, 3D animation, software, product design, and carefully made physical products.

The work here spans visual design, digital tools, 3D printing, product development, and small-batch handmade items.

What I make

I design and produce tools, parts, and digital assets that solve real problems — whether that means a Blender add-on, a custom 3D printed component, or a product made to fit a specific use case properly.

The aim is simple: make things that are useful, considered, and built with care.

Motion Graphics & 3D Animation

Latentsun also covers motion graphics, 3D animation, and visual design work, with a focus on clean, considered visuals and technically driven creative workflows.

This includes animated graphics, 3D product visuals, procedural design, look development, and visual content for digital projects.

Much of the work combines design, animation, and technical problem-solving — using tools such as Blender, Houdini, and other creative software to build visuals that are both functional and distinctive.

3D Printed Products

Latentsun 3D printed products are designed, printed, and hand-finished in the UK. Each item is made in small batches, with attention given to fit, finish, and everyday usability.

Because these are hand-finished products, small variations may be present from one item to another. That is part of the process, but every product is checked before dispatch.

Software & Digital Tools

Alongside physical products, Latentsun also develops software tools and digital systems, including creative tools for workflows such as Blender and procedural design.

The focus is on tools that save time, simplify repetitive work, or make creative workflows more flexible.

Made with care

Latentsun is not a mass-production operation. Products are made in small runs, refined over time, and supported directly.

If something is not right, or if you need help with a product, I’ll do my best to make it right.
