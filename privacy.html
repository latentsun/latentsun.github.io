Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Latentsun respects your privacy and aims to handle your personal information clearly and responsibly.

```

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information is collected when you use this website, place an order, or contact Latentsun.

Who we are

This website is operated by Latentsun.

Contact: rob@latentsun.co.uk

Information we collect

When you place an order, contact us, or use the website, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your billing and shipping address
  • Order details
  • Payment status and transaction information
  • Messages or enquiries you send to us
  • Basic website usage information, where applicable

We do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfil orders
  • Arrange delivery
  • Provide customer support
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Manage refunds, returns, or order issues
  • Keep basic business and accounting records
  • Protect against fraud or misuse

Payments

Payments are processed securely by Stripe.

Latentsun does not store your full card details. Stripe may collect and process payment, billing, and checkout information in order to complete transactions, prevent fraud, and meet legal or regulatory requirements.

You can read Stripe’s privacy policy here: https://stripe.com/gb/privacy

Shipping

If you place an order, your name, delivery address, and relevant order details may be shared with postal or courier services so your order can be delivered.

For international orders, some delivery or customs information may also be required for customs declarations.

Legal basis for processing

We process customer information where it is necessary to fulfil an order, respond to an enquiry, comply with legal obligations, keep business records, or pursue legitimate business interests such as fraud prevention and customer support.

How long we keep information

Order and transaction records may be kept for as long as reasonably necessary for accounting, tax, legal, and business record purposes.

General enquiries are kept only for as long as needed to respond and manage any follow-up.

Who we share information with

We only share personal information where necessary, including with:

  • Stripe, for payment processing
  • Postal or courier services, for delivery
  • Website hosting or email service providers, where relevant
  • Professional or legal advisers, if required
  • Authorities or regulators, where legally required

We do not sell your personal information.

International orders

If you order from outside the UK, your information may be used for shipping, customs, import, or delivery purposes. Customs authorities, postal services, or couriers may process this information depending on the destination country.

Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information where applicable
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your personal information

To make a request, contact: rob@latentsun.co.uk

Complaints

If you are in the UK and are unhappy with how your personal information is handled, you can contact the Information Commissioner’s Office:

https://ico.org.uk

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page.
